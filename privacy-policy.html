Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Rahunok mobile application (“App”, “we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use it. Rahunok is a personal wallet for storing your loyalty and discount cards. We are committed to keeping the amount of personal data we process to the minimum necessary.

By using the App you agree to the practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Who we are

The App is provided by the Rahunok team (the “Developer”). For any privacy-related questions you can reach us at support@rahunok.com.

2. Information we process

2.1 Data you provide

  • Loyalty card data — card names, card/barcode numbers, barcode type, colors, notes, and similar details that you add. This data is stored locally on your device in the App’s private storage.
  • Camera input — if you scan a barcode, the camera is used only to read the code on-device. Camera images are not stored or transmitted by us.

2.2 Optional cloud backup

If you enable backup, a copy of your card data is stored in your own personal cloud account — Apple iCloud on iOS or Google Drive on Android. This backup lives in your account, under your control. We do not operate this storage and cannot read your cloud backup. It is governed by Apple’s or Google’s respective privacy policies.

2.3 Data collected automatically

To keep the App stable and to understand how it is used, we rely on the following services:

ServicePurposeExample data
Firebase Crashlytics (Google)Crash and error diagnosticsCrash logs, device model, OS version, app version
Firebase Analytics (Google)Aggregated, anonymous usage statisticsScreen views, feature usage events, approximate app engagement
Firebase Remote Config (Google)Feature configuration and “force update” noticesApp/version identifiers

These services may process device identifiers and technical data. They do not receive the contents of your loyalty cards.

3. How we use information

  • To provide the core functionality of storing and displaying your cards.
  • To back up and restore your data at your request.
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs.
  • To understand aggregate usage and improve the App.
  • To notify you about required updates.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

4. Legal bases (GDPR)

Where the EU General Data Protection Regulation applies, we process data on the basis of: your consent (for optional analytics and cloud backup), performance of a contract (to provide the App you requested), and our legitimate interests (to keep the App secure and stable).

5. Data sharing

We share information only with the service providers listed above (Google Firebase, and Apple/Google cloud storage when you enable backup), acting as processors on our behalf or under your own account. We may also disclose information if required by law. We do not otherwise share your data with third parties.

6. Data retention

Loyalty card data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Cloud backups remain in your personal cloud account until you delete them. Diagnostic and analytics data is retained by the respective providers according to their policies (typically a limited period).

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including the GDPR and similar laws), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to withdraw consent. Because most data is stored locally, you can exercise many of these rights directly:

  • Delete individual cards or all data within the App.
  • Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data.
  • Disable analytics/backup and delete your cloud backup from your Apple/Google account.

For any additional request, contact us at support@rahunok.com.

8. Security

Your card data is stored in the App’s private, sandboxed storage on your device. Communication with cloud and diagnostic services uses encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

9. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will remove it.

10. International transfers

The service providers we use (Google, Apple) may process data on servers located outside your country. These providers rely on appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) for such transfers.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the latest version. Material changes will be communicated within the App or through the store listing.
